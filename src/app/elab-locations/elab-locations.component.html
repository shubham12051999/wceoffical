Berlin

Youthful character and a vibrant cultural scene make this renowned start-up hub a young entrepreneurs dream. eLab Berlin is ideally located in the central part of the city, in the lively, colorful Kreuzberg district. Buzzing with culture, cafés, music and street festivals, Kreuzberg is the home to many startups and co-working spaces.

London

London history may be grounded in Roman times, but it’s always reaching for the future. As a major financial center with a strong flow of venture capital and a leading hub for the arts, you’ll find a high-energy vibe and plenty of eclectic neighborhoods to explore. Home to nearly nine million people from all over the globe, you can hear 300 languages here.
